Lonavala

Lonavala is a popular hill station in Maharashtra, known for its lush greenery, waterfalls, and ancient caves like the Karla and Bhaja caves. It is located in the Sahyadri mountain range, situated between Pune and Mumbai, and is a hub for adventure activities and nature tourism. The best time to visit is during the monsoon season or from September to April for cooler weather.
