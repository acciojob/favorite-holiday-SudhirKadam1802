Lonavala


Lonavala is a beautiful hill station in Maharashtra, known for its lush green valleys, misty mountains, waterfalls, and pleasant weather.

Top 5 Attractions

  1. Bhushi Dam – Popular monsoon spot.
  2. Lohagad Fort – Historic fort.
  3. Tiger’s Leap – Viewpoint.
  4. Rajmachi Point – Sunset point.
  5. Karla Caves – Ancient Buddhist caves.

Must-Try Foods

  • Chikki
  • Vada Pav
  • Corn Pakoda
