Lonavala


Lonavala is a beautiful hill station in Maharashtra, known for its lush green valleys, misty mountains, waterfalls, and pleasant weather. It is a perfect weekend getaway attracting nature lovers, trekkers, and adventure enthusiasts.

Top 5 Attractions

  1. Bhushi Dam – A popular monsoon spot known for water overflows.
  2. Lohagad Fort – A historic fort with trekking trails and stunning views.
  3. Tiger’s Leap – A cliff-top viewpoint with panoramic scenery.
  4. Rajmachi Point – Famous for sunsets and valley views.
  5. Karla Caves – Ancient Buddhist cave temples with rock-cut architecture.

Must-Try Foods

  • Chikki
  • Vada Pav
  • Kanda Bhaji
  • Corn Pakoda
  • Misal Pav
